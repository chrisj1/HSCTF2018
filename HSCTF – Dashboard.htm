Why am I eligible for prizes?

Teams are eligible if they have no more than four members, are from the United States, and are composed of middle and highschoolers. We require teams to be from the United States to compete for prizes because we may be unable to ship out the prizes internationally. In addition, HSCTF is a CTF designed by and for highschoolers, and so to follow in the spirit of that, we only allow high- and middleschoolers to compete for prizes.

Can I still compete?

Yes, you can still compete for the top ranks and play along like a normal contestant.
